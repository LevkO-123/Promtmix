Dimensional weight (DIM)

Estimate billable weight by carrier. Real rates vary by service; always check your carrier. Uses common divisors: UPS/FedEx/DHL: 139 in³/lb (5000 cm³/kg); USPS: 166 in³/lb (6000 cm³/kg) for some domestic shipments.
